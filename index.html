Bikeable Brews Craft beer establishments near DC area trails

What is bikeablebrews.com?

Bikeablebrews.com is a guide for Custis, Fairfax County Parkway, Mount Vernon, and Washington & Old Dominion (W&OD) trail users searching for craft brew refreshment along their walk or ride. Bikeablebrews has identified 84 craft brew establishments, including 19 breweries, brewpubs, and brewery-affiliated venues located close to the trails. All establishments featured here have a substantial craft beer menu and serve beer on the premises. Most have several taps.
